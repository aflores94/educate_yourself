Frequently Asked Questions


  1. Who are you?
    2. My name is Andrea Flores. I'm an anti-racist, native Texan, and data analyst.
    I created this website out of immense anger, sadness, and frustration after the murders of Breonna Taylor, Ahmaud Arbery, and George Floyd.

  2. How do I submit a resource?
    3. If you want to submit a resource, please use the form here. You will recieve an email when it's added to the site. Thank you for contributing to our efforts!

  3. How can I help?
    4. If you are a software developer (back end in particular) or a social media guru and are interested in volunteering your time to help grow our library please contact me at andrea@educateyourself.io

  4. Do you take donations?
    5. If you would like to donate to Educate Yourself, we ask that you first donate to on the ground community organizations. If you have done so and would still like to donate to us, we so appreciate it!
    You can support us by joining our Patreon community. We're currently exploring options for one-off donations.
    Your donation will go towards covering operating costs and fairly compensating those who give their time and skills to this website.
