Dedicated to Racial Justice

Erasing the excuse of ignorace.



The mission of this website is to act as a megaphone to amplify black voices, and to create a permanent library of anti-racism resources so that no one will ever have the excuse of saying they don't know where to start, or that it's too hard.

It is not the responsibility of Black people to educate you. It is your responsibility to make educating yourself and working to be an anti-racist a part of your everyday life.
